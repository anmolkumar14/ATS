# Zero-to-SRE on Azure (8-Week Training Program)

## Week 1: Introduction to SRE  
**Content:** Overview of Site Reliability Engineering principles, responsibilities, and how it differs from traditional roles.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Dive into the fundamentals of SRE. Understand the importance of reliability, observability, and automation in modern systems.

## Week 2: Azure Fundamentals  
**Content:** Overview of Azure services, concepts, and solutions.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Learn how to start working with Azure, from managing resources to deploying applications.

## Week 3: Monitoring and Alerting  
**Content:** Best practices for monitoring services and setting up alerts.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Understand the tools available in Azure for monitoring and how to configure alerts effectively.

## Week 4: Incident Management  
**Content:** Processes and tools for handling incidents and outages.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Learn the SRE approach to incident management, including post-mortems and blameless cultures.

## Week 5: Automation and Tooling  
**Content:** Automating tasks using scripts and Azure services.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Explore how automation reduces toil and improves reliability in cloud environments.

## Week 6: Capacity Planning  
**Content:** Strategies for planning resource capacity and cost management.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Learn how to analyze usage patterns and forecast demands for your services.

## Week 7: Performance Tuning  
**Content:** Techniques for optimizing the performance of applications.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Discover ways to enhance service performance while maintaining reliability.

## Week 8: Review and Future Trends  
**Content:** Recap of the training and insights on future trends in SRE.  
**Anmol Explanation:** Discuss the evolution of SRE and emerging tools and practices that will shape the future of reliability engineering.
